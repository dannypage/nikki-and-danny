Flying

You can travel to the area via the following airports:

  • Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport (DCA)
  • Washington Dulles International Airport (IAD)

We strongly recommend flying into DCA if possible. It is very close to the event site and your hotel will be easily accessible by Metro or shuttle.

IAD is also a good choice, as it is 30 minutes away.


Metro Rail and Bus

If you are flying in and choose to take the Metro instead of a shuttle to get to your hotel, here are some tips:

  • The Rosslyn Metro stop is on the Orange/Silver/Blue Line.
  • If coming from Reagan National Airport, you can take the Blue Line train towards Largo Town Center, and get off at Rosslyn.
  • If coming from Dulles International Airport, a shuttle/bus will take you to the Silver Line (Wiehle-Reston East). You can then take the Silver Line towards Largo Town Center and get off at Rosslyn.
  • The MetroBus 5A (DC-Dulles Line) will also go directly to Rosslyn.
